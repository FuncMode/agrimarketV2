How It Works

Paano Gumagana ang AgriMarket

Gumagana ang AgriMarket sa pamamagitan ng verified at role-based workflow upang matiyak ang seguridad at tiwala ng lahat ng user.

1

Registration at Verification

  • Ang buyers at sellers ay kailangang mag-register
  • Required ang government-issued ID at actual selfie
  • Manual approval ng admin bago makapag-transact
2

Product Listing (Sellers Only)

  • Verified sellers lamang ang pwedeng mag-post ng produkto
  • Seller-controlled pricing (farm-gate price)
  • May malinaw na product at location details
3

Product Browsing at Ordering (Buyers)

  • Pwede mag-browse kahit hindi verified
  • Verified buyers lamang ang pwedeng mag-place ng order
  • May cart, delivery option, at order confirmation
4

Payment at Delivery

  • Cash-on-Delivery (COD) lamang sa MVP
  • Delivery coordination sa pamamagitan ng messaging at map tools
5

Issue Handling

  • May formal issue reporting
  • Admin-mediated review at resolution
